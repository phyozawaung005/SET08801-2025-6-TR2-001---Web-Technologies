📖 How to Play

WordFall is a fast-paced typing challenge designed to improve speed and accuracy. Words will continuously fall from the top of the screen, and your objective is to type each word correctly before it reaches the bottom.

🕒 60s

Total game time

❤️ 3 Lives

Don't miss words!

🚀 Speed

Increases every 10s

⌨️ Game Controls

  • Watch: Keep your eyes on the falling words.
  • Type: Use your keyboard to type the word exactly.
  • Enter: Words clear automatically on match (or press Enter).
  • Oops: Use Backspace to fix any typos.

📈 Tracking Progress

Your performance is measured using Score, WPM (Words Per Minute), and Accuracy. Try to beat your high score and reach the top of the leaderboard!
