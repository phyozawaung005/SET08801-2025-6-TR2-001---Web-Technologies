How to Play

WordFall is a fast-paced typing challenge designed to improve speed and accuracy. Words will continuously fall from the top of the screen, and your objective is to type each word correctly before it reaches the bottom.

The game lasts for 60 seconds or until all lives are lost. As time progresses, the falling speed increases, making the challenge more intense. Different modes (Kid, Adult, and Gamer) adjust word difficulty and visual style.

Your performance is measured using Score, Words Per Minute (WPM), and Accuracy percentage. A results summary will appear at the end of each session.

Game Controls

  • Type using your keyboard
  • Press Enter to submit a word
  • Use Backspace to correct mistakes
  • Click Restart after game over to play again
