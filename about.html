About WordFall

Our Game

WordFall is a browser-based typing game where words fall from the top of the screen and players must type them before they reach the bottom. Improve your reflexes while enjoying distinct Kid, Adult, and Gamer themes.

Controls

• Type using your keyboard
• Words clear instantly on correct typing
• Backspace to correct mistakes
• High scores are saved locally to your browser.
