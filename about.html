About WordFall

Our Game

WordFall is a browser-based typing game where words fall from the top of the screen and players must type them before they reach the bottom. The game includes Kid, Adult, and Gamer modes with different word difficulty levels and visual styles.

Keybinds

• Type using your keyboard
• Enter to submit a word
• Backspace to correct mistakes
• Restart button after game over

Game Stats

The game tracks Score, Words Per Minute (WPM), Accuracy, and Time Remaining. After each session, a results summary shows overall performance.
